COUMPUTER SCIENCE AND ENGINEERING(CSE)

Department of Computer Science and Engineering has been successfully functioning since 2007. It offers B.Tech (Computer Science and Engineering) and M.Tech (Computer Science and Engineering).

The department of CSE as a whole aims at the development of Ace Computer Science Professionals with ethical values & societal concern.

Our students are placed in various top MNCs like Infosys, IBM, Tech Mahindra, Accenture, Mind Tree, Samsung R&D, Amazon, DBS, EPAM etc., for deserving & esteemed packages of more than 2.4 Lakhs to 18 Lakh per Annum.
